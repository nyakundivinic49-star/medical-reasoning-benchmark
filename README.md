# Clinical Reasoning and Safety Benchmark

## Project Overview

This repository provides a structured benchmark for assessing how effectively AI systems respond to clinical and healthcare-related scenarios.

The focus is on practical reasoning, safety awareness, prioritization, and detection of unsupported medical claims rather than basic medical knowledge recall alone.

---

## Project Objectives

* Assess clinical reasoning in AI-generated responses
* Evaluate recognition of urgent or high-risk patient findings
* Review safety awareness in healthcare-related outputs
* Identify hallucinated, incomplete, or unsafe medical statements
* Compare model performance using consistent scoring frameworks

---

## Benchmark Domains

This project includes scenarios related to:

* Critical Care
* Emergency Care
* Adult Nursing Practice
* Sepsis Recognition
* Shock and Hemodynamic Instability
* Respiratory Compromise
* Patient Safety and Escalation

## Assessment Criteria

AI responses are evaluated using the following dimensions:

* Medical Accuracy
* Urgency and Risk Recognition
* Clinical Prioritization
* Patient Safety Awareness
* Reasoning Strength
* Communication Clarity
* Unsupported Claim Risk
