# Clinical AI Benchmark Report

## Overview

This project presents a structured benchmarking framework for evaluating AI performance in healthcare-related reasoning tasks. The benchmark emphasizes clinical decision-making, recognition of patient deterioration, risk assessment, and safety-conscious recommendations.

Rather than focusing solely on factual recall, the framework assesses how effectively AI systems apply clinical knowledge in realistic healthcare scenarios.

---

## Benchmark Modules

The evaluation suite currently includes:

### Clinical Deterioration Assessment

Focuses on recognition and management of conditions such as sepsis and septic shock.

### Hemodynamic Instability Analysis

Evaluates reasoning related to shock recognition, prioritization, and intervention planning.

### Respiratory Compromise Evaluation

Assesses identification of respiratory emergencies and appropriate escalation strategies.

---

## Scenario Collections

Benchmark cases are organized into the following categories:

* Critical Care Scenarios

## Assessment Framework

Model outputs are evaluated using complementary scoring systems:

### Clinical Decision-Making Framework

Measures:

* Medical correctness
* Risk identification
* Action prioritization
* Clinical reasoning quality
* Patient safety considerations

### Healthcare Safety Framework

Measures:

* Emergency recognition
* Safety of recommendations
* Escalation guidance
* Reliability of information

---

## Benchmark Insights

### Factors Associated with Strong Performance

AI systems generally achieve higher scores when prompts clearly define:

* Professional role or perspective
* Required response structure
* Safety expectations
* Escalation requirements
* Constraints against unsupported assumptions

### Common Failure Patterns

Lower-performing responses frequently:

* Underestimate urgent clinical situations
* Delay appropriate intervention
* Present uncertain information as fact
* Omit critical safety guidance
* Introduce unsupported medical statements
